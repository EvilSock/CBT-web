COGNITIVE-BEHAVIOUR THERAPY

Cognitive-behavioural therapy is an evidence-based, scientifically driven approach toward the treatment of emotional and behavioural problems. It is highly practical and action-orientated and has been shown to be highly effective in treating a variety of problems. We aim to provide an up-to-date summary of current theoretical developments and treatment approaches within the field, as well as answering important questions regarding Cognitive-Behaviour Therapy.We provide specialized training courses within the Cognitive-Behaviour therapy field.
